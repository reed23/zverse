About Us ZVerse is a applications company focused on accelerating the creation of meaningful 3D printable content. With the LAYR 3D Content Creation Platform, ZVerse is creating a suite of software applications that make any 2D content 3D printable. Since being founded in 2012, ZVerse's technology has brought meaningful, high-quality 3D printed content to brands and organizations all over the world. ZVerse is making the world's content 3D printable.
